Welcome to the School of Computer Studies











The School of Computer Studies (SCS) is committed to three main goals: teaching, research, and community service.



Following the Chief tradition of excellence, SCS offers rigorous undergraduate programs that emphasize hands-on learning. Through these programs, students gain practical skills in computer science, and information technology, preparing them for leadership roles in industry, academia, and government.



Additionally, SCS is dedicated to advancing knowledge in the fields of Computer Science and Information Technology through research initiatives. Finally, the college also strives to make a positive impact on communities in need through various service projects.





"Our school was originally conceived as an institution dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, no matter where the trail may lead, no matter how unpopular the result. Thus, reason and science, not tradition and dogma, have always shaped our policies, and thus we have channeled our meager efforts without deviation or break"

- Florentino Cayco Sr. (Founder)
