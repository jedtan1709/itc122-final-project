What is Arellano University Jose Abad Santos Campus?





Arellano University


Arellano University distinguishes itself as an institution committed to catering to the demands of the global workforce. Beyond fostering academic excellence, the university places a premium on aligning its curriculum with industry requisites, practical experience, and professional advancement for its student body.



In pursuit of this objective, Arellano University has cultivated partnerships with prominent local corporations across diverse sectors. These collaborations serve as conduits for students to access pertinent opportunities within their respective fields of study.



Moreover, through its International Programs, Arellano University has established affiliations with esteemed foreign universities, spanning locations such as the United States, Australia, Japan, and Thailand. This strategic alliance underscores the university's commitment to providing equitable access to education of international caliber. Arellano University remains dedicated to continuously refining its offerings in accordance with the evolving needs of its students and the dynamic job market.



Jose Abad Santos Campus


Arellano University Jose Abad Santos Campus is proud to be a home to diverse students from different backgrounds, cultures, and walks of life, fostering a vibrant and inclusive campus community.



As part of the Arellano University system, the Jose Abad Santos Campus shares in the institution's commitment to academic excellence, integrity, and service to the community. Additionally, the campus offers a diverse range of undergraduate and graduate programs, providing students with opportunities for holistic development and professional growth.


Beyond the classroom, the campus offers various extracurricular activities, student organizations, and community engagement initiatives, providing students with opportunities for personal growth and social responsibility.
